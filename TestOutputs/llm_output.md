## Product Mix Optimization MILP\n\n**Objective:** Maximize total profit.\n**Decision Variables:** $x_i$ = quantity of product $i$ to produce.\n**Parameters:** $p_i$ (price), $c_i$ (cost), $m_i$ (molding time), $a_i$ (assembly time), $M_i$ (max capacity), $C_m$ (total molding mins), $C_a$ (total assembly mins).\n**Constraints:**\n1. Molding capacity limit.\n2. Assembly capacity limit.\n3. Market demand and non-negativity.\n4. Integrality of production quantities.\n\nThis model determines the optimal product mix to maximize profit while respecting resource constraints and market limits.
